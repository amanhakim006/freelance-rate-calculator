Why You Need a Precise Hourly Rate?

Freelancing gives you freedom, but it also means you are responsible for every expense. When you work a 9-to-5 job, your employer covers health insurance, electricity, internet, and paid leaves. As a freelancer, your hourly rate must cover all of these plus your profit margin.

Many beginners simply copy the rates of others on Upwork or Fiverr. This is a mistake. Your living costs in Mumbai might be different from someone in New York or Bangkok. This calculator helps you reverse-engineer your rate based on your actual financial needs.
