Common Mistakes

  • Charging for 40 hours/week (you will likely only bill 25-30 hours).
  • Forgetting to add 20-30% extra margin for Taxes.
  • Not planning for sick days or family emergencies.

Pro Tips

  • Start Higher: It's easier to lower your rate than to raise it later.
  • Review Quarterly: As your portfolio grows, increase your income goal.
  • Use Local Currency: Always calculate expenses in your local currency.
