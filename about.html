About Us

Welcome to the Freelance Rate Calculator. Our mission is simple: to help freelancers, designers, and developers stop undercharging for their work.

Freelancing offers freedom, but financial planning can be tricky. Many creatives guess their rates based on competitors, often ignoring their own taxes, software costs, and need for time off.

Who Built This?

This tool was designed and developed by Aman Hakim, a creator who understands the challenges of the gig economy. We believe that every freelancer deserves to earn a sustainable living wage that covers not just their bills, but their future goals as well.

Why Use This Tool?

  • It's 100% Free.
  • It supports International Currencies.
  • It respects your privacy (data stays on your device).
